Angela's Recipe for the best Cinnamon Roll

Ingredients

for the dough:
  • 1/2 cup of warm milk
  • 2 1/2 teaspoun yeast
  • 1/2 of granded sugar
  • 1 egg + 1egg yolk
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3 cup bread flour
For the filling
  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 11/2 teaspoun ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Activate Yeast
    In a bowl, combine warm milk and yeast. Let sit for 5–10 minutes until foamy.
  2. Make Dough
    Mix in sugar, egg, egg yolk, and melted butter.
    Add flour and salt. Mix and knead until smooth (about 8–10 minutes by hand or 5 minutes with a dough hook).
    Transfer to a greased bowl, cover, and let rise for 1–1.5 hours or until doubled in size.
  3. Prepare Filling
    Mix brown sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl.
    Roll dough into a 14×9-inch rectangle.
    Spread softened butter on top, then sprinkle cinnamon sugar mixture evenly.
  4. Roll & Cut
    Roll the dough tightly from the long edge into a log.
    Cut into 12 equal rolls and place in a greased 9×13-inch baking dish.
  5. Second Rise
    Cover and let the rolls rise for 30–45 minutes until puffy.
  6. Bake
    Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
    Bake for 20–25 minutes, until golden brown.
  7. Make Icing
    Beat cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar, vanilla, and salt until smooth.
  8. Frost & Serve
    Spread icing over warm rolls. Serve immediately!
